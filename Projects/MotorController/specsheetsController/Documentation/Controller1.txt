Initialize
01. Select instrument connection ports (USB serial, oscilloscope, and waveform generator).
02. Scan test sample barcode.
03. Fill out form with user information, vendor information, and order information.

Software
01. Connect harness to sample under test at points:
	a. Motor + Connector
	b. Motor - Connector
	c. Neutral Connector
	d. Line Connector
	e. Incline Header
	f. Console Header(if 12-pin header, connect 4-pin UART_Read to last 4 pins of the header)
	g. Tach Sense Header(if controller part number is 359336, DO NOT CONNECT!)
	h. Incline Sense Header
02. Turn on the controller power switch.
03. Scan test sample barcode.

Secondary
01. Toggle SW1.
02. Click begin to test console side voltage.

Motor
01. Toggle SW2, SW3, SW4, and SW5.
02. Set speed potentiometer to the middle position.
03. Click begin to test motor voltages.
04. When prompted, set speed potentiometer to the full counter clockwise (Left) position and click OK.
05. When prompted, set speed potentiometer to the full  clockwise (Right) position and click OK.

Incline
01. Toggle SW6.
02. Click begin to test incline relays.
